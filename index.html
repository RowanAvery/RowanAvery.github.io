About Aaron

My name is Aaron, and I draw things.

I love drawing things that I see and experience every day. Sometimes I draw cartoons about my experiences with ADHD and anxiety. Or about being a queer person in an increasingly homophobic and transphobic world. And sometimes I just draw the things that make me happy. God willing, sometimes my cartoons are even funny!

I was a featured cartoonist at the Kentucky Kernal, University of Kentucky’s award-winning student run newspaper, where nearly a hundred of my cartoons were published. In more recent years I’ve been featured on Forward Kentucky, the Commonwealth’s progressive voice, as well as at The Debrief, where “science and science fiction intersect”.

When I’m not drawing, I love making people laugh. I’ve been on an improv comedy team for the last 5 years, and recently performed my first stand up set. It doesn’t matter to me if you’re laughing with me or at me, just so long as you’re laughing.

Please check me out on Instagram, and if you like what you see, it would mean a lot if you followed me. Maybe like or comment on some of my posts! I thrive on external validation.
